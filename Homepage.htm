ST. Paul Christian High School is a Christian based school located in Mityana along Kampala road.

It was founded in the year 1980 and has been running for over 44 years. Our school was founded by a missionary group aimed at promoting Christian values in young minds.

CO VALUES

Discipline , God fearing, team work, sanitation and good personal hygiene, creativity and problem solving.

GOALS

  1. Creating a God fearing and respectable child

  2. Training and perfecting discipline among learners

  3. Equipping knowledge and critical thinking among learners

  4. Training a child to be problem solving and a good decision maker

  5. Developing students talents and abilities in various sectors
